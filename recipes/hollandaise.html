Hollandaise Sauce

The classic Hollandaise Sauce, good for most green veggies, fish, steak, roast beef, Eggs Benedict, and more!

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks

  • 1/2 lemon, juiced

  • 1 teaspoon cold water

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1/2 cup butter

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together egg yolks, lemon juice, cold water, salt and pepper.

  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Gradually whisk yolk mixture into butter.

  3. Continue whisking over low heat for 8 minutes, or until sauce is thickened. Serve immediately.
